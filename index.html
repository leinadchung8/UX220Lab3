Best Movie

Grown Ups

The movie "Grown Ups" is comedy film based on a group of friends reconnecting together after the sudden lose of their childhood basketball coach. After losing touch with each other growing up, they go through many events during a friends vacation, that bring them and their families close together while delivering a comedy.

After watching this movie many times growing up, "Grown Ups" continues to be a classic movie I keep going back to due to the comedy, action and actors.
